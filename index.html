About Me

I have always had a strong interest in Entrepreneurship, Sustainability, and Renewable energy. Most of my education was obtained at the Santa Fe Community College. Before becoming a Real Estate Broker I worked many years in customer service and the hospitality industry. As New Mexicos Green Collar Broker it is my goal to push for more green residential communities. I hope to one day see more residential communities that promote solar power and geothermal energy. My motive is to help working families save money while leaving the world better then I found it. I work systematically and with a sense of urgency and together we can achieve your real estate goals!
